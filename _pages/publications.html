My first venture goes back to the time when I was 16 years old, when I worked on the project “Making metal balls using terminal velocity “Fall in the liquid”. My paper at that age was accepted and published at 16th physics conference of Iranian students at Chamran University of Kerman, and I received the best paper award Issued by 9th School Network Conference.

At that time I was also qualified for National Math Olympiad among all students in Tehran, Iran.

All these put a passion inside me for science and innovation. That was when I got to know about biomedical engineering and the opportunity to invent new technologies in healthcare. I believe technology can save lives.

That is why I chose Biomedical Engineering – Bioelectric as my bachelor's major, and I graduated ranked 3rd out of 61 students in the faculty.

I could say my next venture was immigration from Iran to Germany in my early twenties. I wanted to work with state-of-the-art technologies and have the opportunities beyond where I was born to reach my goals and full potentials.

During my master I got the passion for AI in diagnosis in medicine and I did multiple research assistances and internships in the field. One of my best projects is My master’s thesis, where I applied deep learning techniques to improve the segmentation of brain structures for the diagnosis of Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s diseases. In another project I developed and implemented self-supervised learning and transfer learning techniques to reduce the need for large labeled datasets in computer vision applications, especially in domains like medical imaging. In this project, I achieved 85% accuracy with only 2,000 labeled images (vs. 90% with 50,000 labeled images in classical supervised learning). My venture during my Master was during a course called “Solution Design In Medical Engineering”, where my teammates and I received both the “best design award” and “best team award”. In this Project we developed a smart walking aid for people with vision problem, in which if the distance is less than 20 cm vibrator alarms the person.
