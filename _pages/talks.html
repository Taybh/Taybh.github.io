I am a speaker and a content creator at Innovate Hamburg.
INNOVATE (Iranian Network of Visionaries And Technology Entrepreneurs) is a global decentralized non-profit organization hosting tech-oriented events. Entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, mentors, and startup-related businesses unite to exchange insights. This initiative fosters collaboration between Iranian tech professionals and North American companies, forging valuable connections in the tech industry. The main organization is INNOVATE Decentral, which has different branches in various parts of the world, and Innovate Hamburg is one of them, covering the north of Germany.
In this community, we engage in interactive discussions, learn about cutting-edge technologies, and make meaningful connections that fuel our entrepreneurial journey.
Moreover, my YouTube channel and Instagram account are another venture of mine, where I share my insights and showcase new technologies worldwide. My audience ranges from the USA to Germany to Iran. I share this content in Innovate Hamburg to reach a wider audience, and I also give talks in this community about tech and AI, sharing my experiences with others, including students and young professionals shaping their paths.
